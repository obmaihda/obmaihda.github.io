Mental Health

Mental Health is a topic most of us shy away from. This could be due to insufficient knowledge or could be lack of knowledge all together.

Our main goal is to bring awareness to topics such as depression and self- awareness. It takes a lot of energy and time when we get into a slump and when we want to get out of it. Health Sawa will be a bridge where we'll be able to be productive and creative in the slumps.
